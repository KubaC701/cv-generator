# CoderDojo Mentor

**Type:** Volunteer
**Date:** September 2023 — present
**Organization:** CoderDojo Berlin

## Overview

Weekly volunteer mentor teaching web development fundamentals to teenagers (ages 13-17). Designed a 12-week curriculum covering HTML, CSS, and JavaScript basics through hands-on projects.

## Highlights

- Mentored 40+ students across 4 cohorts
- 3 students went on to win a regional hackathon
- Created open source teaching materials used by other chapters
